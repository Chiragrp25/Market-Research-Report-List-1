<p><p>The Integrated Video Surveillance System market consists of two main types: Network Video Recorder (NVR) System and Digital Video Recorder (DVR) System. NVR systems use IP cameras and network connectivity to record and store video footage. They offer high-quality video recording, remote accessibility, and advanced features for surveillance management. On the other hand, DVR systems use analog cameras and are typically used in traditional CCTV setups. They store video footage on a hard disk drive and offer basic surveillance capabilities. Both systems play a vital role in ensuring effective video surveillance for various applications.</p></p>
