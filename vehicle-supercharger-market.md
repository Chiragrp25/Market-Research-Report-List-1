<p><strong>The Vehicle Supercharger Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Roots Supercharger</li><li>Twin-Screw Supercharger</li><li>Centrifugal Supercharger</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The vehicle supercharger market consists of three main types: roots supercharger, twin-screw supercharger, and centrifugal supercharger. Roots superchargers are known for their simple design and high efficiency, but they can generate more heat. Twin-screw superchargers offer improved efficiency and have a lower heat generation compared to roots superchargers. On the other hand, centrifugal superchargers are driven by a turbine and provide higher boost levels at higher engine speeds, making them suitable for high-performance vehicles. Each type has its own advantages and considerations for different vehicle applications.</p></p>
