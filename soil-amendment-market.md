<p><strong>The Soil Amendment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Soil Amendment</li><li>Granular Soil Amendment</li><li>Liquid Soil Amendment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The soil amendment market is categorized into three types: powder, granular, and liquid. Powder soil amendments are finely ground materials that can be easily mixed with soil, enhancing its structure and nutrient content. Granular soil amendments are larger particles that are spread over the soil surface and slowly release nutrients as they break down. Liquid soil amendments are solutions that can be sprayed directly onto plants or applied to soil to provide an immediate nutrient boost. These different types cater to the diverse needs of gardeners and farmers in improving the quality of their soil.</p></p>
