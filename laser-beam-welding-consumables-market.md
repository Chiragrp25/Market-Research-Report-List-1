<p><p>Laser-beam welding consumables market types include stick electrodes, solid wires, flux-cored wires, submerged arc welding (SAW) wires, and fluxes. Stick electrodes are welding rods that provide a protective coating during the welding process. Solid wires are single-strand wires used in high-speed welding applications. Flux-cored wires have a hollow core filled with flux, which provides a shielding gas for the welding process. SAW wires are used in submerged arc welding, while fluxes are used to provide protection and deoxidization during the welding process. These consumables play a crucial role in laser-beam welding operations.</p></p>
