<p><p>The halogen-free flame retardant nylon market includes various types such as red phosphorus, melamine salts, and others. Red phosphorus is a widely used flame retardant that functions by releasing phosphoric acid when exposed to heat, thus preventing the spread of fire. Melamine salts, such as melamine polyphosphate, are another common type that releases nitrogen gas when heated, creating a barrier against flames. Other types of halogen-free flame retardants include inorganic compounds like aluminum hydroxide and magnesium hydroxide, which release water vapor and absorb heat to inhibit combustion.</p></p>
