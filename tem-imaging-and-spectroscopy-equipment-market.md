<p><p>The TEM imaging and spectroscopy equipment market can be divided into various types, including EELS (Electron Energy Loss Spectroscopy), EFTEM (Energy Filtered Transmission Electron Microscopy), and others. EELS is a technique used to analyze the energy loss of electrons passing through a sample, providing information on elemental composition and electronic properties. EFTEM utilizes an energy filter to obtain high-resolution images and elemental maps. Other types of TEM equipment may include STEM (Scanning Transmission Electron Microscopy), which enables high-resolution imaging and analysis of samples. These technologies play a crucial role in various scientific and research applications.</p></p>
