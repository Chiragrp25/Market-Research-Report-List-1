<p><p>Vertical Surge Tanks (VST) are commonly used in oil and gas industries for fluid storage and control. Single compartment VST consists of one storage chamber, designed for applications where only one type of fluid is stored. On the other hand, twin compartment VST consists of two separate chambers, each storing a different type of fluid. This allows for easy management and segregation of different fluids. The choice of market types depends on the specific needs and requirements of the industry, ensuring efficient storage and control of fluids.</p></p>
