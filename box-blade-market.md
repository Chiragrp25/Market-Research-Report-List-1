<p><p>Box blades are agricultural implements used for leveling and grading soil. They are available in different market types, including front-tine and rear-tine designs. Front-tine box blades have tines or blades located in front of the main frame, which allows for more precise control when working close to obstacles. Rear-tine box blades, on the other hand, have tines or blades located behind the main frame, providing better visibility of the work area. Both types offer their own advantages, catering to different user preferences and specific tasks in the field of agriculture.</p></p>
