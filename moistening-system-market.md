<p><p>The moistening system market consists of two major types: automatic and semi-automatic systems. An automatic system is designed to automatically sense the moisture levels and adjust the water flow accordingly. It requires minimal human intervention and is suitable for large-scale applications. On the other hand, a semi-automatic system requires some level of human intervention to control the water flow manually. This type of system is more cost-effective and suitable for smaller-scale applications. Both types of systems serve the purpose of providing efficient moisture control for various industrial processes.</p></p>
