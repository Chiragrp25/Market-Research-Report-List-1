<p><strong>The Automotive Adaptive Cruise Control System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Laser-Based</li><li>Radar-Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive adaptive cruise control system market is categorized into laser-based and radar-based systems. Laser-based systems utilize laser beams to measure the distance between vehicles, allowing for accurate detection of the vehicle in front. Radar-based systems, on the other hand, use radio waves to determine the distance and speed of surrounding vehicles. Both systems facilitate adaptive cruise control, which automatically adjusts the vehicle's speed to maintain a safe distance from the car ahead, providing enhanced safety and convenience for drivers.</p></p>
