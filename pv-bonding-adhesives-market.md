<p><p>PV bonding adhesives market types include epoxy-based adhesive, polyurethane-based adhesive, silicon-based adhesive, and others. Epoxy-based adhesive offers high bond strength and durability, making it suitable for various PV applications. Polyurethane-based adhesive provides excellent flexibility and resistance to harsh environments. Silicon-based adhesive offers good adhesion to both PV modules and substrates, along with excellent temperature resistance. Other types may include acrylic-based adhesives or UV-curable adhesives, which offer unique properties based on their chemical composition. These adhesive types are widely used in the PV industry for bonding PV modules, frames, and other components to ensure reliable and long-lasting performance.</p></p>
