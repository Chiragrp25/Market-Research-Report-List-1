<p><p>The two-screw pumps market consists of two main types: the double-suction twin-screw pump and the single-suction twin-screw pump. The double-suction twin-screw pump is designed with two inlets and outlets, allowing for high volume flow rates and more efficient operation. On the other hand, the single-suction twin-screw pump has a single inlet and outlet, making it suitable for lower flow rates and less demanding applications. Both pumps are used in various industrial sectors for handling different types of liquids, offering reliable and effective performance.</p></p>
