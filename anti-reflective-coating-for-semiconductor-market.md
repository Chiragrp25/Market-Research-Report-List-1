<p><strong>The Anti-reflective Coating for Semiconductor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bottom Anti-reflective Coating(BARC)</li><li>Top Anti-reflective Coating(TARC)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The anti-reflective coating for semiconductors is divided into two types: Bottom Anti-reflective Coating (BARC) and Top Anti-reflective Coating (TARC). BARC is applied on the bottom layer of the semiconductor material to reduce light reflection and prevent interference during the photolithography process. On the other hand, TARC is used on the top layer to enhance the performance of the photoresist layer. Both coatings help to improve the accuracy and efficiency of semiconductor manufacturing by minimizing light reflection and improving contrast during the lithography process.</p></p>
