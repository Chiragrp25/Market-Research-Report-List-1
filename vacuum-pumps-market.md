<p><p>Vacuum pumps are categorized into different types based on their functioning mechanisms. Dry vacuum pumps operate without the need for any fluids or lubricants, making them suitable for clean environments. Liquid ring vacuum pumps utilize a rotating liquid ring to create the required vacuum pressure. These pumps are commonly used in applications where large volumes of liquid are present. Rotary vane vacuum pumps utilize rotating vanes to create vacuum pressure. They are widely used in industries requiring low to medium vacuum levels and are known for their compactness and efficiency.</p></p>
