<p><p>Large flake graphite is a type of graphite that is categorized based on its particle size. Flake graphite (+50 Mesh) refers to graphite particles that can pass through a 50 mesh screen but not through a 80 mesh screen. Flake graphite (+80 Mesh) refers to graphite particles that can pass through a 80 mesh screen. These particles are larger and have fewer impurities, making them suitable for applications that require higher purity and larger flake sizes. The "Other" market category includes graphite particles that do not fall into the +50 Mesh or +80 Mesh categories and may have different particle sizes or impurity levels.</p></p>
