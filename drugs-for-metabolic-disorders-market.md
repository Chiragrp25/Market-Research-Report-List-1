<p><p>The drugs for metabolic disorders market can be categorized into different types based on the specific disorder they target. Amino acid metabolism drugs are another category that focuses on treating disorders caused by errors in the breakdown or synthesis of amino acids. Lastly, the "other" category encompasses drugs for various metabolic disorders not included in the previous types.</p></p>
