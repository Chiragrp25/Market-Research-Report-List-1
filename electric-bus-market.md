<p><p>The Electric Bus market consists of three primary types: Battery Electric Bus, Plug-in Hybrid Electric Bus, and Fuel Cell Electric Bus. Battery Electric Bus runs solely on battery power, eliminating the need for any other fuel source. Plug-in Hybrid Electric Bus combines battery power with a small internal combustion engine, allowing for longer range and reduced reliance on charging infrastructure. Fuel Cell Electric Bus uses hydrogen fuel cells to generate electricity, emitting only water vapor. Each of these market types offers a more sustainable and environmentally-friendly alternative to traditional diesel-powered buses.</p></p>
