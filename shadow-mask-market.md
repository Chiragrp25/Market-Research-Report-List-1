<p><p>Shadow mask is a thin metal plate with a fine pattern of holes or slits used in color CRT (cathode ray tube) displays to separate the different electron beams. <p><p>The Shadow Mask market consists of two main types: Fine Metal Mask and Open Mask. This technology is commonly used in manufacturing color CRT displays. On the other hand, Open Mask is another type of shadow mask that utilizes an open grid structure, allowing for higher resolution and better image quality. Open Mask is commonly found in advanced display technologies such as OLED and LCD screens.</p></p>
