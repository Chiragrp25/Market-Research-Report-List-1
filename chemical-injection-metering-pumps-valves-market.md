<p><p>The chemical injection metering pumps and valves market is categorized into three types: diaphragm, piston/plunger, and others. Diaphragm pumps use a flexible diaphragm to displace the chemical. They are preferred for low flow rates and provide accurate dosage control. Piston/plunger pumps employ a reciprocating motion to transfer the chemical, making them suitable for high-pressure applications. The "Others" category includes pump types like rotary, progressive cavity, and peristaltic pumps, which offer unique features depending on specific requirements and chemical properties. These different pump types enable precision and controlled injection of chemicals across various industrial processes.</p></p>
