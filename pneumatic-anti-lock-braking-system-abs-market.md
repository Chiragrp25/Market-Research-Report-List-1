<p><p>The Pneumatic Anti-Lock Braking System (ABS) market consists of three main types: Electronic Control Unit (ECU), Solenoid Modulator Valve, and Wheel Speed Sensor. The ECU is responsible for processing information from various sensors and controlling the ABS. The Solenoid Modulator Valve regulates brake pressure, preventing wheel lock-up. Meanwhile, the Wheel Speed Sensor measures individual wheel speeds to assist in the control of braking pressure. All these components work together to enhance safety and provide efficient and effective braking systems in vehicles.</p></p>
