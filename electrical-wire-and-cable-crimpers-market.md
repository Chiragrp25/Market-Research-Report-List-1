<p><p>Electrical wire and cable crimpers are tools used to create a secure electrical connection between wires and connectors. There are two main market types: insulated and uninsulated. Insulated crimpers are designed to work specifically with wire connectors that have an insulation covering. They ensure a proper electrical connection while also safeguarding against any potential electrical shocks. On the other hand, uninsulated crimpers are used for wire connectors without any insulation covering, usually in applications where electrical insulation is not required. Both types of crimpers serve different purposes in the electrical industry, depending on the specific needs of the connection being made.</p></p>
