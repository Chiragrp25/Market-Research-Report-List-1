<p><p>Vibrating fluid bed dryer market types include constant force system and constant displacement system. In a constant force system, a predetermined force is applied to the bed, ensuring even distribution of particles and uniform drying. On the other hand, a constant displacement system maintains a consistent displacement, which helps in controlling the fluidization and drying process. Both systems have their unique characteristics and are suitable for different applications, providing efficient and effective drying solutions for various industries.</p></p>
