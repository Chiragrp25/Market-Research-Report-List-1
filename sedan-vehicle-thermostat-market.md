<p><p>The sedan vehicle thermostat market consists of different types, namely, the insert thermostat and the housing thermostat. The insert thermostat is a type of thermostat that is installed directly into the engine block or cylinder head. It helps regulate the engine temperature by controlling the flow of coolant through the radiator. On the other hand, the housing thermostat is a type of thermostat that is integrated into the housing unit of the engine. It performs the same function as the insert thermostat, regulating coolant flow and maintaining optimal engine temperature.</p></p>
