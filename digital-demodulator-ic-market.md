<p><p>The Digital Demodulator IC market consists of two types: Single Channel and Dual Channel. </p><p>A Single Channel Digital Demodulator IC is designed for processing a single input signal, making it suitable for applications that require demodulation of a single channel of data or information. It is commonly used in devices like set-top boxes, radios, and satellite receivers.</p><p>On the other hand, a Dual Channel Digital Demodulator IC is capable of processing two input signals simultaneously. This makes it ideal for applications that involve demodulation of two separate channels of data or information, such as multi-channel audio systems or dual tuner televisions. </p><p>Both types of digital demodulator ICs play a crucial role in converting modulated signals into their original form for further processing or utilization.</p></p>
