<p><p>The Aircraft Maintenance, Repair, and Overhaul (MRO) market is divided into three main types: maintenance, repair, and overhaul. Repair refers to fixing any specific issues or damages found during maintenance or arising from accidents. Overhaul, on the other hand, involves a thorough assessment and extensive restoration of the aircraft's major components, such as engines or landing gear, to extend its operational life. These segments collectively cater to the maintenance needs of all types of aircraft.</p></p>
