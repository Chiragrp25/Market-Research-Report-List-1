<p><p>The Crack Sealer market is divided into two main types: Cold Pour Crack Sealer and Hot Pour Crack Sealer. Cold Pour Crack Sealer is applied at ambient temperatures and does not require heating before use. It is commonly used for repairs on small cracks in asphalt pavement. On the other hand, Hot Pour Crack Sealer needs to be heated to a specific temperature before application, ensuring better adhesion and longer-lasting results. This type is typically used for larger cracks and offers enhanced durability in high-traffic areas.</p></p>
