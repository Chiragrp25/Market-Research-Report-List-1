<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Laser printer toner can be categorized into two main types: magnetic and non-magnetic. Magnetic toner contains iron oxide particles that are attracted to the printer's drum through an electrostatic charge. This type of toner is commonly used in high-speed printing and photocopying applications. On the other hand, non-magnetic toner does not contain iron oxide particles and is usually used in lower-speed printers, providing high-quality prints with vibrant colors. Both types of toners offer different benefits and functionalities based on their specific characteristics and intended uses.</p></p>
