<p><p>Collaborative modular robotics refers to the market for customizable and flexible robot systems that can work collaboratively with humans. Articulated modular robots consist of multiple joints, allowing for complex movements. Cartesian modular robots use a rectangular coordinate system to move along three axes. SCARA modular robots have horizontal joints, making them ideal for assembly tasks. Parallel modular robots have multiple limbs that work in parallel to achieve high-speed and precise movements. The collaborative modular robots market encompasses all these types, providing solutions for various industries requiring human-robot collaboration in tasks such as assembly, packaging, and logistics.</p></p>
