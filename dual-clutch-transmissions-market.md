<p><p>Dual clutch transmissions (DCT) are advanced automatic transmissions that provide seamless gear shifting. They are classified into two market types based on the type of clutches used. Wet multi-plate clutches utilize an oil bath to keep the clutch plates cool and lubricated, resulting in improved performance and durability. On the other hand, dry single-plate clutches do not rely on oil, making them lighter and more compact. Both types offer advantages depending on the specific application, with wet multi-plate clutches being ideal for high-performance vehicles and dry single-plate clutches suitable for smaller vehicles or hybrids.</p></p>
