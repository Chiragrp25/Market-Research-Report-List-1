<p><p>The digital smart set-top box (STB) market comprises three main types: digital cable, terrestrial digital, and satellite digital. The digital cable market refers to STBs used in cable television systems, connecting the cable signal to the user's television set. Terrestrial digital STBs are used in over-the-air broadcast systems, receiving and decoding digital signals from terrestrial transmitters. Satellite digital STBs, on the other hand, receive and decode digital signals from satellites, allowing users to access a wide range of satellite television channels.</p></p>
