<p><p>Sponge rubber materials are used in various applications due to their unique properties. Closed-cell rubber refers to a type of sponge rubber with sealed cells, which provides excellent insulation and waterproofing characteristics. Open-cell rubber, on the other hand, has interconnected cells that allow air and water to pass through, making it more suitable for cushioning and absorbing impact. Other market types include expanded rubber, which involves the heating and expansion of rubber sheets or extrusions, and recycled rubber, where rubber waste is processed into sponge rubber materials for sustainability purposes.</p></p>
