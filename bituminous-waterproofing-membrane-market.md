<p><p>Bituminous waterproofing membrane is a type of material used in construction to provide waterproofing and weatherproofing to structures. The growing construction industry, especially in emerging economies, is driving the demand for waterproofing materials. Additionally, stricter building codes and regulations aimed at improving energy efficiency and reducing environmental impact are further boosting the demand for bituminous waterproofing membranes.</p><p>Another factor driving the market growth is the increasing adoption of innovative technologies in the development of waterproofing materials. <p><p>Bituminous waterproofing membranes are widely used to provide a protective barrier against water infiltration in various construction applications. There are different types of bituminous membranes available in the market, including APP (Atactic Polypropylene) Modified, SBS Modified, and Self-Adhesive Membranes. </p><p>APP Modified membranes are manufactured by blending bitumen with APP polymers, enhancing their resistance to UV radiation and heat. SBS Modified membranes, on the other hand, are made from bitumen mixed with SBS polymers, offering excellent flexibility and cold temperature performance. Self-adhesive membranes provide ease of installation as they come with a pre-applied adhesive layer on one side, eliminating the need for heating or torching during application.</p></p>
