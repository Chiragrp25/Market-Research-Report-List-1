<p><p>The light metal baler market consists of two main types: the manual and automatic balers. Manual balers require human intervention to operate, where the user manually feeds the materials into the machine and controls the baling process. These balers are typically suitable for small-scale operations or low volumes of lightweight metal materials. On the other hand, automatic balers are equipped with advanced technology and do not require constant monitoring.
