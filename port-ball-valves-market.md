<p><p>Port ball valves are widely used in various industries and are classified into two main types: soft seal valves and hard seal valves.</p><p>1. Soft seal valves have a sealing element made of materials like rubber or PTFE, which provides excellent sealing performance. These valves are typically used for low-pressure applications and fluids that may contain particles or impurities. Soft seal valves are easy to operate and require less torque to open and close.</p><p>2. Hard seal valves, on the other hand, use materials like metal or ceramic for sealing, which provides a highly durable and reliable sealing performance. These valves are suitable for high-pressure and high-temperature applications and fluids that are corrosive or abrasive. Hard seal valves have a longer lifespan and require higher torque for operation.</p><p>In summary, soft seal valves are ideal for low-pressure and particle-contaminated fluids, while hard seal valves are suitable for high-pressure, high-temperature, and corrosive/abrasive fluids.</p></p>
