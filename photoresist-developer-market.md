<p><p>Photoresist developer is a chemical solution used in the photolithography process to develop photoresist coatings on semiconductor wafers. Positive-type photoresist developer is designed to selectively remove the exposed areas of positive photoresist, whereas negative-type photoresist developer removes the unexposed areas of negative photoresist. Positive-type photoresist is commonly used for high-resolution imaging, while negative-type photoresist enables simpler patterning processes. Both market types are essential in semiconductor manufacturing, catering to different requirements in terms of resolution, ease of use, and specific manufacturing processes.</p></p>
