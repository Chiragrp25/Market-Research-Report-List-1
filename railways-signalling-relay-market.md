<p><p>Railways Signalling Relay Market Types can be categorized into two main types: DC (Direct Current) market and AC (Alternating Current) market. </p><p>In the DC market, signaling relays are designed for use in applications where direct current is the primary source of power. DC signaling systems are often used in older railway networks where the infrastructure and equipment are not equipped to handle AC power. </p><p>On the other hand, the AC market refers to signaling relays that are designed to operate with alternating current power sources. AC systems are more commonly used in modern railway networks as they allow for higher power transmission efficiency and are more versatile in terms of integrating with modern signaling technology.</p></p>
