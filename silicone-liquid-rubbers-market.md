<p><ul><li>One-component</li><li>Two-component</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Silicone liquid rubbers are used for various applications, and they can be classified into two types based on their curing mechanism: one-component and two-component. One-component silicone rubbers are pre-mixed formulations that cure when exposed to moisture in the air, providing convenience and ease of use. On the other hand, two-component silicone rubbers consist of a base and a curing agent that require mixing before application. This type offers higher performance and a broader range of properties as the curing process can be customized to meet specific requirements.</p></p>
