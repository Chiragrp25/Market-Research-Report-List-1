<p><p>Hydraulic lime is a type of lime binder that is derived from limestone containing a sufficient amount of clay minerals. <p><p>Hydraulic Lime refers to a type of lime derived from limestone that has the ability to harden and set underwater. The market for Hydraulic Lime is divided into three types, namely NHL2, NHL3.5, and NHL5. NHL2 is the softest and fastest-setting type, suitable for low-strength applications such as pointing. NHL3.5 is the most commonly used type, balancing strength and setting time, suitable for general construction purposes. NHL5 is the strongest and slowest-setting type, ideal for underwater applications and areas with high exposure to moisture.</p></p>
