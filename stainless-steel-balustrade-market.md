<p><p>The stainless steel balustrade market is divided into two types: interior railings and exterior railings. Interior railings are installed inside buildings and are used mainly for staircase balustrades, balcony railings, and handrails. These railings enhance the aesthetic appeal of the interior space while providing safety and support. On the other hand, exterior railings are installed outside buildings and serve purposes such as balcony railings, terrace railings, and garden fencing. Both types of railings are made of stainless steel, which offers durability, strength, and corrosion resistance.</p></p>
