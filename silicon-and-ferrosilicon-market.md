<p><p>Silicon is a chemical element with the symbol Si and atomic number 14. It is a hard, brittle crystalline solid with a blue-grey metallic lustre. It is a tetravalent metalloid and a semiconductor. Silicon is used extensively in computer chips and solar panel production due to its ability to efficiently conduct electricity.</p><p>On the other hand, ferrosilicon is an alloy made of iron and silicon. It is produced by reduction of quartzite or sand with coke in the presence of iron. Ferrosilicon is used as a deoxidizer and alloying element in the production of steel. It also serves as a reducing agent in the production of ferroalloys and silicon metal.</p><p>The global market for silicon and ferrosilicon is projected to witness significant growth in the forecast period.
