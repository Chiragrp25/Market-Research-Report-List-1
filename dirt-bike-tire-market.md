<p><p>The dirt bike tire market is divided into three types: pneumatic, solid, and polyurethane. Pneumatic tires are filled with air, providing a smoother ride with the ability to absorb shocks and bumps on rough terrains. Solid tires are made of solid rubber, eliminating the risks of flats and punctures, but sacrificing some comfort and grip. Polyurethane tires offer a combination of the benefits of both pneumatic and solid tires, offering good shock absorption, durability, and improved traction on various surfaces.</p></p>
