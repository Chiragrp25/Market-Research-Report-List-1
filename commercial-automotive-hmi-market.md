<p><p>The Commercial Automotive HMI (Human-Machine Interface) market can be classified into two main types: Standard HMI and Multimodal HMI. Standard HMI refers to the traditional approach where the interaction between the driver and the vehicle is mainly through physical buttons, knobs, and a touchscreen display. On the other hand, Multimodal HMI is an advanced system that incorporates multiple modes of interaction, such as voice commands, gesture controls, and eye-tracking technology, to enhance the user experience and provide more intuitive and convenient ways for drivers to interact with their vehicles.</p></p>
