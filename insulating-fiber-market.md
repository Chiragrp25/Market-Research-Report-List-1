Fiberglass insulation is made from fine glass fibers and is widely used due to its high thermal resistance. Mineral wool, produced from volcanic rock or slag, offers excellent fire resistance. Cellulose insulation is made from recycled paper and is eco-friendly. Plastic fiber insulation is made from plastic resins and offers good moisture resistance. Natural fibers, like cotton or wool, provide sustainable insulation options. Other types of insulating fibers include polyurethane foam, polystyrene, and polyisocyanurate.</p></p>
