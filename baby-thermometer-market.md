<p><p>The baby thermometer market consists of three main types: infrared thermometers, ear temperature guns, and intelligent thermometers. Infrared thermometers use infrared technology to measure the temperature on the baby's forehead or body without touching them. Ear temperature guns are designed to measure the baby's temperature by inserting a probe into their ear canal. Intelligent thermometers are equipped with advanced features such as Bluetooth connectivity and smartphone apps, offering parents the ability to track, store, and analyze their baby's temperature data conveniently.</p></p>
