<p><p>Cell culture serum is used in laboratories for the growth and maintenance of cells. The market for cell culture serum is primarily divided into three types: bovine serum, fetal bovine serum (FBS), and other serum. Bovine serum is derived from adult cows and is commonly used as a nutrient source for cell cultivation. Fetal bovine serum, derived from the blood of unborn calves, is highly rich in growth factors and promotes cell growth. Other serum includes serum derived from other animal sources and synthetic serum substitutes. These different types cater to the diverse needs of cell culture applications.</p></p>
