<p><strong>The Cryorefrigerators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Gifford-Mcmahon Cryorefrigerators</li><li>Pulse-Tube Cryorefrigerators</li><li>Stirling Cryorefrigerators</li><li>Joule-Thomson Cryorefrigerators</li><li>Brayton Cryorefrigerators</li><li>Other</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cryorefrigerators are categorized into different market types based on their operating principles. Gifford-McMahon Cryorefrigerators use a piston-like mechanism to compress and expand gases, achieving cooling effects. Pulse-Tube Cryorefrigerators utilize temperature fluctuations in a gas-filled tube to create cooling. Stirling Cryorefrigerators work by cyclically compressing and expanding a fluid, resulting in cooling. Joule-Thomson Cryorefrigerators employ a throttling process that reduces gas pressure, leading to cooling. Brayton Cryorefrigerators use a gas turbine cycle to cool gases. Lastly, there are also other market types that are not specifically classified under these primary categories.</p></p>
