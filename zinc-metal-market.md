<p><p>Zinc metal is a lustrous, bluish-white metal that is commonly used in various industries due to its excellent corrosion resistance properties. It is primarily produced from zinc ore, which is extracted from mines and then refined through a process called smelting to obtain the pure metal. Zinc metal is utilized in the production of various products, including galvanized steel, alloys, brass, zinc oxide, and batteries, among others. The automotive, construction, and infrastructure sectors are the major end-users of zinc metal.</p><p>The zinc metal market is expected to experience significant growth during the forecast period. Factors driving this growth include the rising demand for galvanized steel in the construction and automotive industries, increasing infrastructure development activities, and growing urbanization and industrialization globally. Additionally, the surge in demand for zinc-based batteries and the growing adoption of zinc alloys in various manufacturing processes further contribute to the market growth.</p><p>The market analysis reveals that the Asia-Pacific region dominates the zinc metal market, owing to the strong presence of key end-user industries, such as construction and automotive. Rapid industrialization in emerging economies like China and India, coupled with substantial investments in infrastructure, drive the market in this region. North America and Europe also contribute significantly to the market share due to the growing demand for galvanized steel and increasing infrastructure refurbishment projects.</p><p>Furthermore, the market is witnessing several latest trends, such as the shift towards zinc-air batteries for energy storage applications, the development of advanced zinc alloys with improved properties, and the exploration of new zinc deposits to ensure a sustainable supply in the future. As a result of these trends and factors, the zinc metal market is predicted to grow at a compound annual growth rate (CAGR) of 14.4% during the forecast period.</p></p>
