<p><p>The plastic corrugated pipe market consists of two main types: single wall corrugated and double wall corrugated. Single wall corrugated pipes are made from a single layer of plastic and are typically used for non-pressurized applications such as drainage systems. Double wall corrugated pipes, on the other hand, are made from two layers of plastic, with a smooth inner layer and a corrugated outer layer. These pipes are mainly used for underground drainage and sewage systems, offering enhanced strength and durability.</p></p>
