<p><p>The automotive electric door lock market is divided into two main types: electromagnetic door locks and electric motor door locks. </p><p>Electromagnetic door locks utilize an electric current to generate a magnetic field, which then locks or unlocks the door electronically. This type of lock provides high-security level and is widely used in luxury vehicles and commercial applications. </p><p>On the other hand, electric motor door locks use a small electric motor to physically move the locking mechanism, providing automatic locking and unlocking functionality. This type of lock is commonly found in most passenger vehicles, offering convenience and ease of use.</p></p>
