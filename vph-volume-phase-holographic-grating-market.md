They are widely used in various applications such as spectroscopy, astronomy, and telecommunications. On the other hand, HOEs are optical elements that utilize holographic methods for functions like beam splitting, beam steering, and focusing. HOEs find applications in industries like automotive, healthcare, and entertainment, offering advantages such as lightweight design and compactness.</p></p>
