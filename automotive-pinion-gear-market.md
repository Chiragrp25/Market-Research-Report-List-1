<p><ul><li>Bevel Gear</li><li>Helical Gear</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive pinion gear market encompasses various types, including bevel gears and helical gears. Bevel gears are used in applications where the torque needs to be transferred between non-parallel and intersecting shafts. They have cone-shaped teeth that allow smooth and efficient power transmission. Helical gears, on the other hand, have angled teeth that gradually engage, resulting in less noise and vibration compared to other gear types. They are suitable for high-speed applications as they can transmit power smoothly and evenly. Both gear types play crucial roles in the automotive industry.</p></p>
