<p><p>Industrial X-ray testing machines are used to inspect and analyze the internal structure of various materials and products. Hard X-ray machines utilize high-energy X-rays to penetrate dense materials like metals, allowing for detailed imaging and detection of defects. Soft X-ray machines, on the other hand, use low-energy X-rays to examine less dense materials such as composite materials, plastics, and biological samples. Both types of machines play a crucial role in quality control, non-destructive testing, and ensuring the safety and reliability of industrial products.</p></p>
