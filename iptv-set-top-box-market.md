<p><strong>The IPTV Set-Top Box Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>OTT (Over the Top)</li><li>DTT (Direct Terrestrial Transmission)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>IPTV set-top box market types include Over the Top (OTT) and Direct Terrestrial Transmission (DTT). OTT refers to the distribution of content over the internet directly to the viewer, bypassing traditional broadcasting channels. It allows users to access a wide range of multimedia services on their televisions. On the other hand, DTT refers to the transmission of television signals through terrestrial (land-based) broadcast networks. DTT requires the use of an antenna and can provide access to free-to-air channels and sometimes subscription-based services.</p></p>
