<p><p>The Polyurethane Waterproof Coating market comprises three types: Polyurethane Emulsion Coating, Polyurethane Dispersion Coating, and Polyurethane Aqueous Solution Coating. Polyurethane Emulsion Coating refers to a water-based coating that forms a protective layer upon drying. Polyurethane Dispersion Coating involves the dispersion of polyurethane particles in a water-based medium and provides excellent waterproofing. Polyurethane Aqueous Solution Coating, as the term suggests, is a solution-based coating where polyurethane is dissolved in water to form a water-resistant film. These coatings are widely used in various industries for their waterproofing properties.</p></p>
