<p><p>The solid carbide milling cutter market is classified into three types: straight shank, taper shank, and others. Straight shank milling cutters have a cylindrical shape and are held in the machine tool's chuck. Taper shank milling cutters have a tapered shape that matches the machine tool's spindle, providing better stability and alignment. The "others" category comprises milling cutters with unique designs or specialized applications. These categorizations are based on the different shank types used, each offering specific advantages for various machining operations.</p></p>
