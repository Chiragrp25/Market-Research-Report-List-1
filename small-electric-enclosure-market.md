<p><p>The small electric enclosure market consists of three main types: wall-mounted enclosures, floor-mounted/free-standing enclosures, and underground enclosures. Wall-mounted enclosures are fixed directly on walls and are suitable for compact spaces. Floor-mounted/free-standing enclosures are standalone units placed on the floor and provide larger storage capacity. They are popular in industrial settings. Underground enclosures, as the name suggests, are installed below ground level and are designed to protect electrical equipment in outdoor environments. These three types cater to different needs and are essential for housing electrical components.</p></p>
