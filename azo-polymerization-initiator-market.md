<p><p>The Azo Polymerization Initiator market is categorized into two types: Water Soluble and Oil Soluble initiators. Water soluble initiators are those that can dissolve in water and are commonly used in aqueous polymerization processes. They provide stable and efficient polymerization reactions in water-based systems. On the other hand, oil soluble initiators are designed to be easily dispersible in oil-based systems. These initiators are used in oil-based polymerizations, enabling efficient and controlled reactions in such systems. Both types offer different benefits and are chosen based on the specific requirements of the polymerization process.</p></p>
