<p><p>Insulin needle-free syringes are medical devices used for insulin delivery without the need for conventional needles. The market can be categorized into two types: powder injections and liquid injections. Powder injections involve the use of insulin in powdered form, which requires reconstitution with a diluent, typically before administration. On the other hand, liquid injections utilize pre-filled cartridges or vials containing liquid insulin, eliminating the need for reconstitution. Both types offer a needle-free alternative for insulin delivery, providing convenience and potentially reducing the fear and pain associated with needles.</p></p>
