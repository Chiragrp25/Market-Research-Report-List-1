<p><p>Dimethyl carbonate (DMC) is an organic carbonate compound with the chemical formula CH3OC(O)OCH3. It is a colorless, flammable liquid with a sweet odor and is highly soluble in water. DMC is primarily used as a chemical intermediate for the production of various chemicals such as polycarbonate, polyurethane, and methyl isobutyl ketone (MIBK). It is also used as a solvent in industries such as paints and coatings, adhesives, and pharmaceuticals.</p><p>The dimethyl carbonate solvent market is expected to witness substantial growth during the forecast period, primarily driven by the increasing demand for environmentally friendly solvents and the growing demand from end-use industries.
