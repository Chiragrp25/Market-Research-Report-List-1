<p><p>The aviation actuation systems market comprises three types of drive systems: hydraulic drive system, electric drive system, and pneumatic drive system. The hydraulic drive system utilizes fluid power to generate mechanical force and movement for controlling various aircraft components. The electric drive system relies on electric power to generate the necessary force and motion. On the other hand, the pneumatic drive system uses compressed air to generate the required force and motion for actuating aircraft systems. These drive systems are crucial in ensuring precise and efficient control of aircraft components.</p></p>
