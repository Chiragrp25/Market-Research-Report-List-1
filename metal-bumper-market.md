<p><p>The metal bumper market consists of two main types: front bumper and rear bumper. Front bumpers are installed on the front end of vehicles and are designed to absorb impact during collisions, protecting the engine and other crucial components. Rear bumpers, on the other hand, are installed on the back end of vehicles and provide protection in case of rear-end collisions. Both front and rear bumpers are made of metal, usually steel or aluminum, offering durability and ensuring the safety of the vehicle and its occupants.</p></p>
