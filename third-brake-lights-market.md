<p><p>Third brake lights, also known as center high-mounted stop lamps (CHMSL), are a crucial safety feature installed in vehicles. This additional warning helps to minimize the risk of rear-end collisions and improve overall road safety.</p><p>The global third brake lights market has been witnessing steady growth in recent years.
