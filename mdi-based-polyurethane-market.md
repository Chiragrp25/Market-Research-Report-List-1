<p><p>MDI Based Polyurethane refers to a type of polyurethane that is produced using methylene diphenyl diisocyanate (MDI) as a key component. The market for MDI Based Polyurethane can be divided into two main types - Pure MDI and Polymeric MDI (PMDI). Pure MDI refers to a form of MDI that contains predominantly 4,4'-MDI isomers, while PMDI is a mixture of MDI isomers and polymeric MDI. Both types are widely used in various industries such as construction, automotive, furniture, and insulation due to their excellent properties and versatility.</p></p>
