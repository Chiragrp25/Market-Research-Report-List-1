<p><strong>The Fluorinated Polyimide Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Perfluoropolymer Type</li><li>Partially Fluorinated Polymer Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fluorinated Polyimide market consists of two main types: Perfluoropolymer Type and Partially Fluorinated Polymer Type. Perfluoropolymer Type refers to a type of Fluorinated Polyimide that contains fully fluorinated aromatic rings in its polymer structure. This type offers exceptional thermal stability, chemical resistance, and low surface energy. Partially Fluorinated Polymer Type, on the other hand, contains both fluorinated and non-fluorinated segments in its polymer structure. This type provides a balance between good mechanical properties and improved processability, while still maintaining some of the desirable properties of fluorinated polyimides.</p></p>
