Hybrid solid-state LiDAR combines mechanical and solid-state components to improve performance and reduce cost. Solid-state LiDAR eliminates moving parts, offering smaller size, higher reliability, and lower cost. These different types of LiDAR technologies cater to various automotive applications and customer requirements.</p></p>
