<p><p>Triacetate cellulose film is available in two types: Short cotton-based and wood pulp-based. Short cotton-based triacetate cellulose film is produced by using short cotton fibers as the raw material. It offers high transparency, excellent dimensional stability, and good mechanical strength, making it suitable for various applications like packaging and printing. On the other hand, wood pulp-based triacetate cellulose film is made from wood pulp fibers and provides similar characteristics as the short cotton-based type. Both types have found wide usage in the market due to their superior properties and versatility.</p></p>
