<p><p>Wireless Broadband System market consists of three types: Fixed Wireless Broadband, Mobile Wireless Broadband, and Satellite Wireless Broadband. Mobile Wireless Broadband enables internet connectivity on the go, allowing users to access the internet anywhere using smartphones or portable devices. Satellite Wireless Broadband utilizes satellites to provide internet access in remote or rural areas where traditional wired connections are not available.</p></p>
