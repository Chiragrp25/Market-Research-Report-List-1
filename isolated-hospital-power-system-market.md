<p><strong>The Isolated Hospital Power System Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>IT system</li><li>TN-S system</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Isolated Hospital Power System market includes two types of systems: IT system and TN-S system. The IT system refers to a power distribution system in which all power sources and equipment have their own isolated grounding system. This ensures a high level of reliability and prevents electrical faults from affecting other parts of the system. On the other hand, the TN-S system refers to a power distribution network in which the neutral and protective earth conductors are separated throughout the entire system. This allows for better protection against electrical faults and improves the safety of the power supply in hospitals.</p></p>
