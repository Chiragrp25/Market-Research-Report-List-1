<p><p>Rotary Union Market Types refer to the different designs and configurations of rotary unions used for transferring fluids or gases in industrial applications. Multi-Passage rotary unions are equipped with multiple passages that allow the simultaneous transfer of multiple media without mixing. They are suitable for applications requiring the transfer of different substances. On the other hand, Single-Passage rotary unions have only one passage, enabling the transfer of a single medium at a time. They are commonly used in applications where only one fluid or gas needs to be transferred.</p></p>
