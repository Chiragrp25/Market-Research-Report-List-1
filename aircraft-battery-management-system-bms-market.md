<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The Aircraft Battery Management System (BMS) market can be categorized into three types: centralized, modular, and distributed. In a centralized system, a single BMS manages all the batteries in an aircraft, providing a centralized control and monitoring approach. In a modular system, multiple BMS units are used, each managing a specific battery or group of batteries, offering flexibility and redundancy. In a distributed system, individual batteries have their own BMS, enabling independent control and monitoring. These different market types offer varying levels of complexity, cost-effectiveness, and scalability to fulfill diverse aircraft requirements.</p></p>
