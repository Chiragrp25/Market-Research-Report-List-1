<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Optical waveguide displays are divided into two types: channel waveguide and planar waveguide. Channel waveguide contains microscopic channels within a transparent material, which guide the light to propagate. This type offers high light confinement and is commonly used in integrated optics devices. On the other hand, planar waveguide consists of a thin layer of waveguide material on a substrate. It supports the propagation of light in a single plane parallel to the substrate surface, making it suitable for compact and lightweight display applications.</p></p>
