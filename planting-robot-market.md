<p><p>The Planting Robot market consists of two main types: Rear Tow Seeder Robot and Front Mounted Robot. A Rear Tow Seeder Robot is designed to be attached to the back of a tractor and pulled along, evenly spreading seeds as it moves. On the other hand, a Front Mounted Robot is directly mounted onto the front of a tractor and operates by directly planting seeds into the soil. Both types serve the purpose of mechanizing and optimizing the planting process, increasing efficiency and improving yields for farmers.</p></p>
