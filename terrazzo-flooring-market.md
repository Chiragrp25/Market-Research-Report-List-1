<p><p>Terrazzo flooring can be classified into two main types: inorganic terrazzo and epoxy terrazzo. Inorganic terrazzo is made up of materials like marble chips, glass chips, and quartz, which are mixed with cement. It offers a traditional and natural look but requires regular maintenance. On the other hand, epoxy terrazzo is composed of epoxy resin, pigments, and various aggregates. It provides a more vibrant and durable finish compared to inorganic terrazzo. Epoxy terrazzo is resistant to chemicals, stains, and moisture, making it suitable for high-traffic areas.</p></p>
