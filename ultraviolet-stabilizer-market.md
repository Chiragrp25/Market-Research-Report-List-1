<p><p>The Ultraviolet Stabilizer market includes various types of stabilizers used to protect products from the harmful effects of UV radiation. UV Absorbers (UVA) are compounds that can absorb UV light and convert it into heat, reducing the impact on the product. Hindered Amine Light Stabilizers (HALS) are chemicals that can scavenge free radicals produced by UV radiation, preventing them from damaging the product. Other types of stabilizers include antioxidants, which can inhibit the degradation of polymers caused by UV exposure. These stabilizers play a crucial role in increasing the lifespan and durability of UV-sensitive materials.</p></p>
