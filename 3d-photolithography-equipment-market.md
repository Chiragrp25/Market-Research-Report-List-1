<p><p>The 3D photolithography equipment market consists of two major types: Ultraviolet (UV) Laser and Extreme Ultraviolet (EUV) Laser. UV Laser uses a laser light source with a wavelength in the UV range to cure the polymer resin and create intricate 3D structures. EUV Laser, on the other hand, uses a shorter wavelength laser source, which enables even higher precision and resolution in the 3D printing process. Both types offer advanced capabilities for producing complex and detailed objects, catering to various industries like automotive, aerospace, and healthcare.</p></p>
