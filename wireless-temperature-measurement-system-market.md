<p><p>The wireless temperature measurement system market consists of two main types: non-contact type and contact type. The non-contact type measures temperature without physical contact with the object or subject being measured. It uses technologies like infrared or thermal imaging to capture the temperature remotely. On the other hand, the contact type involves direct physical contact with the object using sensors or probes to measure temperature. Both types have their advantages and applications in various industries such as healthcare, manufacturing, and food processing.</p></p>
