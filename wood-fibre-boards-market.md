<p><p>Wood fibre boards, commonly used as construction materials, can be classified into two main types: porous wood fibre boards and hard wood fibre boards. Porous wood fibre boards are characterized by their ability to absorb moisture, making them a suitable choice for areas with high humidity and moisture levels. On the other hand, hard wood fibre boards are dense and durable, making them ideal for applications that require high strength and resistance to wear and tear. These two types cater to different needs and preferences in the market of wood fibre boards.</p></p>
