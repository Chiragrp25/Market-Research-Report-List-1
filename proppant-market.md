<p><p>The proppant market encompasses three main types: frac sand proppant, resin-coated proppant, and ceramic proppant. Frac sand proppant is obtained from natural sand deposits and is commonly used due to its abundance and low cost. Resin-coated proppant consists of a layer of resin applied to the sand particles, enhancing their strength and conductivity. Ceramic proppant, made from bauxite or kaolin clay, offers higher strength and thermal resistance, making it suitable for demanding applications. These proppants are crucial in hydraulic fracturing to keep induced fractures open and allow for efficient oil or gas extraction.</p></p>
