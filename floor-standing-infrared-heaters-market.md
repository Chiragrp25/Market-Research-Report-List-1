<p><p>Floor-standing infrared heaters are available in three different market types - Far, Medium, and Near. Far market heaters emit longer infrared waves that have limited range and are ideal for heating large outdoor spaces such as patios or gardens. Medium market heaters emit medium-length infrared waves that have a moderate range and are suitable for partially enclosed areas like balconies or garages. Near market heaters emit shorter infrared waves that have a shorter range and are best suited for small indoor spaces like bedrooms or offices. These market types cater to different heating needs based on the size and nature of the area to be heated.</p></p>
