<p><p>Bio-Poly Alpha Olefin (PAO) is a type of synthetic lubricant widely used in various industries. The market for Bio-PAO can be categorized into three types based on viscosity levels. Low viscosity PAOs have a thinner consistency and are suited for applications that require quick lubrication, such as compressors and turbines. Medium viscosity PAOs offer a balance between thin and thick viscosity, making them suitable for hydraulic systems and gear oils. High viscosity PAOs are thick and ideal for applications like engine oils, industrial gear oils, and greases that require excellent load-carrying capacity and stability.</p></p>
