MT requires manual gear shifting by the driver, while AT automatically shifts gears as per the vehicle's speed. AMT combines both manual and automatic transmissions. CVT offers smooth gear shifting by using a belt and pulley system. DCT utilizes two separate clutches for quick and seamless gear changes. These different transmission types cater to the diverse preferences and needs of light vehicle users.</p></p>
