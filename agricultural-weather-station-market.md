<p><ul><li>Solar Powered</li><li>AC Powered</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Agricultural Weather Station market can be divided into two types based on their power source: Solar Powered and AC Powered. Solar Powered weather stations use solar panels to generate electricity, making them suitable for remote and off-grid agricultural areas. They offer a sustainable and cost-efficient solution. On the other hand, AC Powered weather stations rely on a direct power connection and are commonly used in areas with stable electricity supply. While they may require higher upfront costs and ongoing energy expenses, they provide a consistent power source for continuous data collection and monitoring.</p></p>
