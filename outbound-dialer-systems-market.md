Predictive dialing is an automated system that uses algorithms to predict when an agent will be available and connects the call accordingly. Preview dialing allows agents to preview customer information before making the call. Power dialing automatically dials a predetermined number of leads per agent, minimizing downtime. These systems improve productivity, increase call volumes, and ensure efficient use of agents' time during outbound calling campaigns.</p></p>
