LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) technology uses lasers to measure distances and create highly accurate 3D maps. Digital orthophotography combines aerial photography with GIS technology to create accurate maps. Other technologies in this market include satellite imagery, mobile mapping, and real-time traffic data. These technologies play a crucial role in navigation systems, autonomous vehicles, and smart city planning for improving efficiency and safety on the roads.</p></p>
