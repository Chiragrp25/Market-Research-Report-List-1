<p><p>The electric car battery charger market is divided into two types: on-board and off-board chargers. On-board chargers are integrated into the electric vehicle (EV) and allow it to be charged directly from an external power source. Off-board chargers, on the other hand, are separate charging units that are not connected to the EV itself. These chargers are typically used in home or public charging stations and are capable of charging multiple EVs simultaneously. Both on-board and off-board chargers play a crucial role in enabling efficient and convenient charging options for electric vehicles.</p></p>
