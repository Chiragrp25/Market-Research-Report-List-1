<p><p>The Ultra High Purity PFA Heat Exchangers market includes two main types: Shell & Tube Type and Immersion Type. Shell & Tube Type involves a shell filled with fluid that surrounds a bundle of PFA tubes. This design allows for efficient heat transfer between two fluids. Immersion Type, on the other hand, uses a PFA heat exchanger immersed directly in a fluid. This type is commonly used when space is limited or when specific parts of a system need to be heated or cooled. Both types play a crucial role in various industries requiring ultra-high purity heat transfer.</p></p>
