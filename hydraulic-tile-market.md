<p><p>Hydraulic tile market includes two major types: Standard Field Tiles and Ridge Tiles. Standard Field Tiles are flat tiles used as a base layer for flooring or wall coverings, providing a smooth and even surface. They come in various sizes, shapes, and designs, allowing for creative combinations. On the other hand, Ridge Tiles are specially designed tiles that are curved or inclined, intended to cover the intersection between two roof slopes. They help to maintain the structural integrity of the roof and create an aesthetically pleasing finish. Overall, both types play crucial roles in the hydraulic tile market.</p></p>
