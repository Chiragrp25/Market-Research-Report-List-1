Base Malts serve as the foundation in beer production, providing the majority of the fermentable sugars and color. They contribute to the overall flavor and body of the beer. On the other hand, Specialty Malts are used in smaller quantities and add unique characteristics such as color, aroma, and flavor to the final product. They offer a wide range of options for brewers to experiment with and create distinctive brews.</p></p>
