<p><p>Intraoperative Neurophysiologic Monitoring (IONM) Services market includes various types of monitoring techniques used during surgeries to assess the function of the nervous system. Electroencephalography (EEG) involves recording brain wave patterns to monitor brain activity. Electromyography (EMG) records electrical activity in muscles, helping evaluate nerve and muscle function. Evoked Potentials (EP) measure electrical responses generated by the nervous system in response to specific stimuli, providing information about sensory and motor pathways. These techniques aid in reducing operative risks and complications, allowing surgeons to make adjustments during surgery to ensure patient safety and optimal outcomes.</p></p>
