<p><p>The 3D Laser Cutting Equipment market offers two main types of lasers: Fiber Laser and Carbon Dioxide (CO2) Laser. Fiber Laser uses a high-power laser beam generated through a fiber optic cable, resulting in precise and efficient cutting of various materials. It offers faster cutting speeds and is often used for high-precision tasks. On the other hand, CO2 Laser uses a gas mixture and electrical stimulation to produce a laser beam. It is commonly used for cutting thick materials, such as metals, and provides excellent cutting quality.</p></p>
