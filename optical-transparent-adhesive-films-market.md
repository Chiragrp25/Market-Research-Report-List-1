<p><p>Optical transparent adhesive films market is categorized into two types: high peeling strength type and low peeling strength type. High peeling strength adhesive films are designed to provide strong bonding and resistance to peeling forces. They are widely used in industries where strong adhesion is required, such as automotive and electronics. On the other hand, low peeling strength adhesive films offer a weaker bond, making them suitable for applications where removal without damage is necessary, like screen protectors or temporary signage.</p></p>
