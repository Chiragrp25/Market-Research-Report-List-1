<p><ul><li>FI</li><li>BI</li><li>DD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Low noise CCD cameras are categorized into three market types: FI (frame interline transfer), BI (frame buried interline), and DD (frame transferred interline) cameras. FI cameras excel in high-speed image capturing and offer low noise performance, making them suitable for applications such as machine vision and industrial inspections. BI cameras have improved light sensitivity and are ideal for low-light conditions or surveillance purposes. DD cameras provide high image quality, specifically designed for professional photography and scientific imaging applications. Each market type caters to specific needs and requirements within the low noise CCD camera market.</p></p>
