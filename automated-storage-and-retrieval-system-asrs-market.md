<p><p>Automated Storage and Retrieval System (AS/RS) is a computerized system that uses either a unit-load or mini-load design to automate the storage and retrieval of goods in a warehouse. In unit-load AS/RS, large and heavy items are stored on pallets or containers, while mini-load AS/RS is designed for smaller items. Unit-load AS/RS offers high storage capacity and is suitable for bulk storage, whereas mini-load AS/RS provides quicker and more precise picking of individual items. Both types improve efficiency and reduce labor costs by automating the storage and retrieval process.</p></p>
