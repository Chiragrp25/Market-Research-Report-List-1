<p><p>The endoscopic baskets market includes various types of baskets used in endoscopic procedures. Straight baskets have a linear design, suitable for removing stones or foreign bodies. Tipless baskets have no distal tip, reducing the risk of trauma during procedures. Mesh baskets have a mesh-like surface, enabling efficient grasping of objects. Helical baskets have a spiral-shaped structure, facilitating the capture of stones or tissue fragments. Other types of baskets may include oval or triangular designs, each with their specific features and applications.</p></p>
