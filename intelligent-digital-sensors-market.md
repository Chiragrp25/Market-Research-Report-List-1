<p><p>Insertion intelligent digital sensors are sensors that are inserted into a process stream or a pipe for measuring and monitoring various parameters such as temperature, pressure, flow, and level. These sensors are designed to provide accurate and real-time data for process control and optimization. Immersion intelligent digital sensors, on the other hand, are sensors that are submerged or immersed in a liquid or fluid for measuring and monitoring parameters such as temperature, pH, conductivity, and turbidity. These sensors are commonly used in industries such as water treatment, food and beverage, and pharmaceuticals for ensuring quality and safety.</p></p>
