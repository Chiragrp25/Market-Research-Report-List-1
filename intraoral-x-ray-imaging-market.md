<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>The intraoral X-ray imaging market consists of various types of X-ray imaging techniques used for dental examinations. Bitewing X-rays capture the upper and lower teeth in a single image, allowing dentists to identify cavities between teeth and evaluate dental alignment. Periapical X-rays focus on a specific tooth, showing its entire structure, including roots, surrounding bone, and nearby teeth. Occlusal X-rays capture a broader view of the upper or lower dental arch, helping dentists detect abnormalities, document tooth development in children, and assess jaw fractures.</p></p>
