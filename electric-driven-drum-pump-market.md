<p><p>Electric driven drum pumps are used to transfer liquids such as chemicals, oils, or solvents from drums or barrels. The market for electric driven drum pumps consists of three main types: gear pumps, screw pumps, and vane pumps. Gear pumps work by using rotating gears to create suction and move the liquid. Screw pumps utilize a helical screw to displace the liquid, while vane pumps use vanes that slide in and out of slots to create flow. These pump types offer different advantages depending on the viscosity and temperature of the liquid being transferred.</p></p>
