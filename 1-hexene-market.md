<p><p>1-Hexene is an organic compound with the chemical formula C6H12. It belongs to the family of linear alpha olefins and is obtained as a byproduct during the production of ethylene and propylene. 1-Hexene is primarily used as a comonomer in the production of high-density polyethylene (HDPE). It improves the flexibility, impact resistance, and processability of HDPE, making it suitable for various applications such as packaging films, bottles, pipes, and wires.</p><p>The global 1-Hexene market is experiencing significant growth and is expected to continue expanding at a CAGR of 6.3% during the forecast period. The increasing demand for HDPE from industries such as packaging, automotive, and construction is driving the growth of the 1-Hexene market.
