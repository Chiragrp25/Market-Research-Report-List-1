<p><strong>The Synthetic Brake Fluid Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>DOT 3</li><li>DOT 4</li><li>DOT 5</li><li>DOT 5.1</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The synthetic brake fluid market consists of various types, including DOT 3, DOT 4, DOT 5, and DOT 5.1. DOT 3 brake fluid is a glycol-based formula suitable for most vehicles, offering a high boiling point and corrosion protection. DOT 4 brake fluid provides similar benefits but with an even higher boiling point. DOT 5 brake fluid is silicone-based, offering excellent resistance to moisture and a stable performance under extreme temperatures. DOT 5.1 brake fluid is a glycol-ether-based formula with properties similar to DOT 3 and DOT 4, but with a higher boiling point.</p></p>
