<p><p>High Performance Rubber Market Types</p><p>The high performance rubber market is divided into two main types: natural rubber and synthetic rubber. Natural rubber is derived from the latex of certain trees, primarily found in tropical regions. It is known for its excellent elasticity and abrasion resistance, making it suitable for various applications such as automotive tires and industrial belts. On the other hand, synthetic rubber is chemically produced and offers a wide range of formulations to meet specific requirements. It is widely used in the production of durable goods, including footwear, seals, and hoses, due to its superior chemical and physical properties.</p></p>
