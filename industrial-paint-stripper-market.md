<p><p>The industrial paint stripper market can be categorized into three main types: alkaline, acidic, and others. Alkaline paint strippers are water-based solutions containing alkaline compounds that work by dissolving the paint layers. Acidic paint strippers, on the other hand, contain corrosive acids that chemically react with the paint to loosen its adhesion. The "others" category encompasses alternative paint stripping methods such as solvents, heat guns, or mechanical methods. These different types of paint strippers cater to varying needs and requirements in the industrial sector, offering a range of options for effectively removing paint coatings.</p></p>
