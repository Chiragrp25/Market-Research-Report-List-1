<p><p>The Anti-collision System market is comprised of various types of technologies, including Radar, Camera, Ultrasound, and LiDAR. Radar systems use radio waves to detect and track objects around the vehicle, offering long-range coverage and accurate distance measurement. Camera-based systems utilize image processing to detect objects and assess their trajectory. Ultrasound systems rely on sound waves to estimate distances and detect obstacles, often used for low-speed maneuvers. LiDAR systems use lasers to create a 3D map of the environment, providing highly accurate and detailed object detection and tracking capabilities.</p></p>
