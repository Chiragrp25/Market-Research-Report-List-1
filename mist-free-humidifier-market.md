<p><p>Mist-free humidifiers are available in two main types: ultrasonic humidifiers and evaporative humidifiers. Ultrasonic humidifiers work by using ultrasonic vibrations to break water into small particles, which are then released as a fine mist into the air. These humidifiers are silent and energy-efficient. On the other hand, evaporative humidifiers function by blowing air over a wet wick or filter, causing the water to evaporate and increase humidity levels. They are budget-friendly and suitable for larger spaces. Both types aim to humidify the air without creating visible mist.</p></p>
