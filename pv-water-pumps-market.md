<p><strong>The PV Water Pumps Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Surface Suction</li><li>Submersible</li></ul></p>
<p><p>PV water pumps refer to water pumping systems that are powered by solar energy. There are different types of PV water pumps, including surface suction and submersible pumps. </p><p>Surface suction pumps are installed above the water source and use suction to draw water up into the system. They are suitable for shallow wells and can pump water to elevated locations. </p><p>On the other hand, submersible pumps are fully submerged in the water source and push water up to the surface. They are used for deeper wells and can provide higher water output. Both types of pumps offer efficient and environmentally friendly solutions for water pumping needs.</p></p>
