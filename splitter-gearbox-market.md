<p><p>A Splitter Gearbox is a type of gearbox used in various industrial applications. It is categorized into two main types: Single Pump Drive and Multi Pump Drive. In the Single Pump Drive market, the gearbox is designed to power a single hydraulic pump, allowing the transmission of power from the engine to the pump. On the other hand, the Multi Pump Drive market involves a gearbox that can power multiple hydraulic pumps simultaneously, enabling the transmission of power to multiple pumps from the engine. Both types cater to different requirements and applications within industries.</p></p>
