<p><strong>The Automotive Starter Motor and Alternator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forced Engagement</li><li>Flexible Engagement</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive starter motor market consists of two types: forced engagement and flexible engagement. In forced engagement, the pinion gear directly engages with the engine's flywheel, ensuring a reliable connection for starting the engine. On the other hand, flexible engagement uses a clutch mechanism that allows the pinion gear to smoothly engage with the flywheel, reducing wear and tear on the gear teeth. Both types provide the necessary power to start the engine, but flexible engagement offers added benefits of reduced noise, vibration, and enhanced durability.</p></p>
