<p><p>Polyethylene film is a versatile material widely used in various industries. There are two main types: high density polyethylene (HDPE) film and low density polyethylene (LDPE) film. HDPE film is known for its strong and durable properties, making it suitable for applications that require superior strength and impact resistance, such as packaging materials and outdoor covers. On the other hand, LDPE film is more flexible and has better clarity, making it ideal for applications like agricultural films, food packaging, and shrink wraps. Both HDPE and LDPE films serve different market needs based on their specific properties.</p></p>
