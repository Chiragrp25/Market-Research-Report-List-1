<p><p>Ammonium thiosulfate (ATS) fertilizer is a popular nitrogen and sulfur fertilizer that is commonly used in agriculture. It contains around 12% nitrogen and 26% sulfur, making it an ideal choice for crops that require both these nutrients.</p><p>The demand for ammonium thiosulfate fertilizer is witnessing substantial growth due to its several benefits. Firstly, it provides a readily available source of nitrogen and sulfur to plants, promoting vigorous growth and higher yields. It also improves the nutrient availability in the soil, making it more efficient for plants to uptake nutrients. Another advantage of ATS fertilizer is its compatibility with other fertilizers and agrochemicals, allowing farmers to apply it in combination with other products.</p><p>In terms of market analysis, the global ammonium thiosulfate fertilizer market is projected to grow at a CAGR of 9% during the forecast period.
