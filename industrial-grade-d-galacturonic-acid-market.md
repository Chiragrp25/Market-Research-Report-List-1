<p><p>Industrial grade D-galacturonic acid is a high-value chemical compound derived from pectin, a plant cell wall polysaccharide. It is extensively used in various applications, including pharmaceuticals, food and beverages, cosmetics, and agricultural industry. D-galacturonic acid exhibits excellent acid-binding capabilities, which make it ideal for applications in pharmaceuticals as it can be used in the formulation of calcium supplements and drugs for treating urinary tract infections.</p><p>In the food and beverages industry, D-galacturonic acid is utilized as an acidulant, pH regulator, and flavor enhancer. It is also used in the production of pectinase enzyme, which is employed in wine clarification and juice extraction. In the cosmetics industry, it is used in skincare products as an exfoliant and moisturizing agent. Moreover, D-galacturonic acid finds applications in the agricultural industry as a plant growth regulator, controlling plant ripening and other physiological processes.</p><p>The global market for industrial grade D-galacturonic acid is projected to grow at a CAGR of 7.6% during the forecast period.
