The growth is primarily driven by the increasing demand for semiconductors in various applications such as electronics, telecommunications, automotive, and healthcare. These furnaces provide precise temperature control, uniform heating, and cooling capabilities required for the annealing processes.</p><p>In terms of trends, the market is witnessing a shift towards the adoption of advanced technologies such as rapid thermal annealing (RTA) and plasma enhanced annealing (PEA).
