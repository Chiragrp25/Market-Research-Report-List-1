<p><p>Car Tyre Inflator market consists of two types, Portable Inflators and Stationary Inflators. Portable Inflators are compact and lightweight devices that drivers can carry in their vehicles for emergencies. They are typically powered by a car's 12-volt socket or a rechargeable battery. Stationary Inflators, on the other hand, are larger and more powerful inflators commonly found in gas stations or car service centers. They are designed for heavy-duty use and can rapidly inflate multiple tires simultaneously. Both types serve the purpose of inflating car tyres but differ in terms of portability and usage scenarios.</p></p>
