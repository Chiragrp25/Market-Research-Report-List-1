<p><p>Vacuum tube solar collectors refer to solar thermal systems that utilize evacuated glass tubes to trap solar energy. There are two main types in the market. Thermal oil type collectors use an oil-based heat transfer fluid, such as synthetic or mineral oil, to absorb and transfer heat from the sun to a heat exchanger. On the other hand, molten salt type collectors use a mixture of molten salts as the heat transfer medium. These collectors efficiently capture and store solar energy, offering diverse options for heat generation and utilization.</p></p>
