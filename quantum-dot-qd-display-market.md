<p><p>The Quantum Dot (QD) Display market encompasses two main types: QDEF and QLED. QDEF, or Quantum Dot Enhancement Film, utilizes a thin layer of quantum dots placed on a backplane of a display to enhance color performance and efficiency. This type of display technology can be used in LCD screens to achieve a wider color gamut and improved brightness. On the other hand, QLED, or Quantum Light Emitting Diode, is a display technology that utilizes quantum dots to emit light directly, offering enhanced color accuracy, contrast, and viewing angles compared to traditional LED displays.</p></p>
