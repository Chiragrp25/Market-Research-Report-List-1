<p><p>Pistol grip sanders are a type of power tool used for sanding surfaces. They can be classified into two market types: single action sanders and double action sanders. Single action sanders have a rotary motion that only allows the sanding pad to move in a circular pattern. On the other hand, double action sanders have both a rotary and an orbital motion, which enables the sanding pad to move in a circular and up-and-down pattern simultaneously. These distinctions in market types offer users different options for achieving desired sanding results.</p></p>
