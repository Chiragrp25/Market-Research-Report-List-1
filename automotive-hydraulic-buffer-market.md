<p><p>The automotive hydraulic buffer market includes two main types: single-tube and double-tube buffers. Single-tube buffers consist of a single cylinder that contains both the gas and liquid components. This design allows for a simple and cost-effective solution. On the other hand, double-tube buffers are made up of two cylinders, one inside the other. The outer cylinder contains gas, while the inner cylinder contains liquid. This design offers more precise control over damping and allows for better overall performance.</p></p>
