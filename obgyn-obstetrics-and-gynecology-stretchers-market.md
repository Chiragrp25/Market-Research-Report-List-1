<p><p>OB/GYN (Obstetrics and Gynecology) stretchers are utilized in medical settings to transport and support pregnant women before, during, and after childbirth. These stretchers can be classified into three types: fixed stretchers, adjustable stretchers, and stretcher chairs. Fixed stretchers are designed with a stationary frame and height, suitable for stable patients who do not require frequent adjustments. Adjustable stretchers offer flexibility with adjustable features such as height and backrest, providing customized support based on patient needs. Stretcher chairs combine the features of both stretchers and chairs, allowing the patient to sit upright or recline during examinations or procedures.</p></p>
