<p>&nbsp;</p>
<p><p>Electrostatic chucks for wafer market includes two main types: Coulomb Type Electrostatic Chucks and Johnsen-Rahbek (JR) Type Electrostatic Chucks. Coulomb type chucks use a conducting electrode and rely on static charge attraction to hold wafers in place during processing. On the other hand, Johnsen-Rahbek type chucks employ an insulator and utilize electrostatic levitation force to secure wafers. Both types offer advantages such as high stability, uniform temperature distribution, and minimal process contamination. These chucks play a critical role in the semiconductor industry by ensuring accurate and efficient wafer handling.</p></p>
