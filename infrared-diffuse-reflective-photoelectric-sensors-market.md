<p><p>Infrared diffuse reflective photoelectric sensors are classified into two market types: digital signal and analog signal. Digital signal sensors provide a binary output signal, which is either ON or OFF, making it suitable for applications that require simple detection or presence/absence sensing. On the other hand, analog signal sensors provide a continuous output signal that varies proportionally with the detected object's distance or intensity, enabling more precise measurements in applications such as distance sensing or edge detection.</p></p>
