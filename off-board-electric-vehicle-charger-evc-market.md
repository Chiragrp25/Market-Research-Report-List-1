<p><p>Off-board Electric Vehicle Chargers (EVCs) are categorized into two types: Level 2 Chargers and Level 3 Chargers. Level 2 Chargers are designed for residential and commercial use, providing a moderate charging rate. They are commonly used as home charging solutions and can fully charge a vehicle overnight. On the other hand, Level 3 Chargers, also known as DC fast chargers, offer a rapid charging option for public infrastructure. They deliver a high voltage and can charge electric vehicles significantly faster, allowing drivers to top up their battery within minutes.</p></p>
