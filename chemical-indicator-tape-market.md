There are different types of chemical indicator tapes available, such as steam sterilization indicator tape and ethylene oxide (EO) sterilization indicator tape. Steam sterilization indicator tape changes color when exposed to high temperatures in steam sterilization cycles. EO sterilization indicator tape changes color when exposed to ethylene oxide gas used in sterilization. These indicators help healthcare professionals ensure that medical equipment and supplies have undergone proper sterilization procedures.</p></p>
