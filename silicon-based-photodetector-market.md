<p><p>Silicon-based photodetector market comprises two significant types: Silicon Drift Detector (SDD) and Silicon Photomultiplier (SiPM). SDDs are highly sensitive detectors used in X-ray spectroscopy, providing excellent energy resolution and low noise levels. On the other hand, SiPMs are capable of detecting single photons and are extensively employed in medical imaging, high-energy physics, and other fields demanding high sensitivity. Both SDD and SiPM technologies cater to different applications in detecting and quantifying light or radiation, serving diverse industries with their distinct features and advantages.</p></p>
