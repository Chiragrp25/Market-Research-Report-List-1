<p><strong>The Vehicle Ultrasonic Radar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forward Radar</li><li>Reversing Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Ultrasonic Radar market consists of two main types: Forward Radar and Reversing Radar. Forward Radar is designed to detect objects in front of the vehicle, allowing for collision avoidance and adaptive cruise control features. It uses ultrasonic waves to measure the distance between the vehicle and obstacles, providing accurate and real-time information to the driver. On the other hand, Reversing Radar is specifically used for parking assistance. It helps drivers navigate tight spaces by detecting objects behind the vehicle and emitting warning signals to prevent collisions. Both radar systems enhance safety and improve overall driving experience.</p></p>
