<p><p>Instrumentation tubes are used in various industries for measuring and controlling fluid flow. There are two main types of instrumentation tubes: seamless tubes and welded tubes. Seamless tubes are manufactured without any seams or welds and offer high corrosion resistance and reliability. On the other hand, welded tubes are made by welding two or more pieces of metal together and offer cost-effectiveness and flexibility. Both types are widely used in industries such as oil and gas, chemical, and petrochemical for applications requiring precise fluid control and measurement.</p></p>
