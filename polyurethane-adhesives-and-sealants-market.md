<p><p>Polyurethane adhesives and sealants are used for bonding and sealing applications. The market types include water-based, solvent-based, hot-melt, and others. </p><p>Water-based adhesives and sealants are environmentally friendly, offer low toxicity, and have high bond strength. Solvent-based ones contain volatile organic compounds and provide quick drying and high bond strength. Hot-melt adhesives and sealants solidify upon cooling, are user-friendly, and have fast setting times. Other types may include moisture-cured or radiation-cured, which have specific curing requirements. Each type has its own advantages and is used in various industries depending on their specific properties and application requirements.</p></p>
