<p><p>The stainless steel gratings market consists of various types, including welded grating, swage locked grating, press locked grating, and others. Welded grating is made by welding bearing bars and crossbars together, providing a sturdy structure. Swage locked grating is assembled by embedding crossbars into pre-punched holes in the bearing bars. Press locked grating is created by pressing crossbars into bearing bars using a high-pressure process, resulting in a tight connection. The "others" category may include types such as riveted grating or close-mesh grating, offering different design options for various applications.</p></p>
