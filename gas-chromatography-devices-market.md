<p><p>The gas chromatography devices market can be classified into two main types: portable gas chromatography devices and stationary gas chromatography devices. </p><p>Portable gas chromatography devices are compact and lightweight, allowing for easy transport and on-site analysis. They are commonly used in field applications, such as environmental monitoring, where samples need to be analyzed quickly and efficiently.</p><p>On the other hand, stationary gas chromatography devices are larger and more powerful, typically used in laboratory settings. They offer higher analytical capabilities and are utilized for detailed and precise analysis of complex samples that require longer analysis times.</p></p>
