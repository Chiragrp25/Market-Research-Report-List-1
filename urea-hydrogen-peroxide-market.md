<p><p>Urea Hydrogen Peroxide (UHP) is a chemical compound that consists of urea and hydrogen peroxide. UHP is widely used for bleaching applications in textiles, as it provides excellent results without damaging the fabric.</p><p>Additionally, the rising environmental concerns and stringent regulations regarding wastewater treatment are fostering the demand for UHP in the market. UHP is an effective oxidizing agent for wastewater treatment, as it helps in the removal of organic pollutants and colorants from water bodies.</p><p>Furthermore, the growing demand for UHP in the pulp and paper industry is also contributing to the market growth.
