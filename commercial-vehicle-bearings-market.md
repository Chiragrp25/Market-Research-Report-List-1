<p><ul><li>Ball Bearings</li><li>Roller Bearings</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Commercial Vehicle Bearings Market includes various types of bearings such as ball bearings and roller bearings. Ball bearings consist of small metal balls between two rings, reducing friction and enabling smooth rotation in commercial vehicles. Roller bearings, on the other hand, use cylindrical rollers instead of balls, offering enhanced load-carrying capacity and stability to withstand heavy loads and extreme operating conditions. Both ball bearings and roller bearings play a crucial role in ensuring efficient and reliable performance of commercial vehicles by reducing friction and enabling smooth rotation of various vehicle components.</p></p>
