<p><p>The plastic tarpaulin market consists of two main types: PVC tarpaulin and PE tarpaulin. PVC tarpaulin is made from a durable type of plastic called polyvinyl chloride and is known for its excellent strength and resistance to UV radiation, chemicals, and fire. On the other hand, PE tarpaulin is made from polyethylene and provides good insulation and waterproofing properties but is less durable compared to PVC. Both types are widely used in various industries for protecting goods, covering surfaces, and providing temporary shelter.</p></p>
