<p><p>Dirt bikes come in various market types: 4-stroke, 2-stroke, and electric. 4-stroke bikes have a more advanced engine design where fuel combustion occurs every four strokes of the piston, providing smoother power delivery and greater fuel efficiency. 2-stroke bikes, on the other hand, have a simpler engine design with combustion occurring every two strokes, resulting in higher power but less fuel efficiency. Electric dirt bikes are gaining popularity, characterized by battery-powered engines that produce instant torque and emit zero emissions, providing a quieter and environmentally friendly riding experience.</p></p>
