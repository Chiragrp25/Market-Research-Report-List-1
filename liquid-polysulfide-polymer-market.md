<p><p>The liquid polysulfide polymer market is categorized into two types: medium-viscosity and high-viscosity. Medium-viscosity polysulfide polymers have a moderate thickness or consistency, making them suitable for applications that require a balance between flowability and stability. On the other hand, high-viscosity polysulfide polymers have a thicker consistency, offering excellent resistance to deformation and high durability. These types of polymers are commonly used in applications where a high level of viscosity is required, such as sealants, adhesives, and aerospace industries.</p></p>
