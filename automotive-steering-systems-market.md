<p><p>The Automotive Steering Systems market encompasses various types of steering systems used in vehicles. These include Electric Power Steering (EPS), Hydraulic Power Steering (HPS), Electro-Hydraulic Power Steering (EHPS), and Manual Steering (MS). EPS employs an electric motor to assist in steering, providing a more efficient and lightweight option. HPS uses hydraulic pressure to aid steering, offering a traditional and reliable solution. EHPS combines electrical and hydraulic power to enhance steering performance. MS, on the other hand, requires the driver's physical effort to turn the wheels. These different systems cater to different vehicle types and driver preferences.</p></p>
