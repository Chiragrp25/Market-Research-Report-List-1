<p><p>Polyester matting agents are widely used in various industries, such as automotive, furniture, and coatings, to provide a matt appearance to the finished products. There are two types of polyester matting agents available in the market, including gel and powder forms. Gel matting agents are typically in a liquid or paste-like form and are mixed directly into the coating or resin system. On the other hand, powder matting agents are finely ground particles that are added to the coating formulation. Both types of matting agents effectively reduce the gloss and give a dull or matte finish to the coated surface.</p></p>
